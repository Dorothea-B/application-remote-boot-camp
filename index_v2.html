APIs

- The waiter's of the computer world.

API is short for Application Programming Interface and it's purpose is to retreive information from somewhere and send it over to somewhere else.

One example I found likened it to a restaurant. The guest sits there with a menu of items; the waiter is the messenger transferring your food request back to the kitchen. Then returns/delivers your request back to you.

This is an essential part of the internet, enabling us to collect data from various databases and returning it to the end user. There are different kinds of APIs, as well. Read more about it here.
